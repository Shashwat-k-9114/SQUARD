About Squard Talent

Squard Talent is a specialist healthcare workforce and care-solutions company, supporting providers across aged care, disability, allied health, community health, and clinical settings throughout Australia.

We partner with healthcare organisations to deliver qualified professionals, flexible workforce solutions, and tele-consult services that support continuity of care, compliance, and positive outcomes for patients, participants, and providers.

Our approach goes beyond filling shifts. We focus on long-term workforce reliability, regulatory compliance, and service quality, acting as an extension of our clients' teams.

Tele-Consult & Telehealth Services

Squard Talent offers tele-consult and telehealth workforce solutions, enabling providers and participants to access professional support remotely.

Tele-consult services may include:

  • Mental health and counselling support
  • Wellbeing and psychosocial consultations
  • Clinical and care-related advisory services

Tele-consult solutions are designed to:

  • Improve access to care, particularly in regional and remote areas
  • Support early intervention and continuity of care
  • Complement in-person service delivery

All tele-consult services are delivered by appropriately qualified professionals and aligned with relevant regulatory and clinical standards.

Compliance & Quality Assurance

All workforce and tele-consult professionals engaged through Squard Talent meet appropriate regulatory and organisational requirements, which may include:

  • Right to Work in Australia
  • National Police Checks
  • NDIS Worker Screening / Working with Vulnerable People checks
  • Relevant qualifications and professional registrations (including AHPRA where applicable)
  • Role-specific credentialing and scope-of-practice checks
  • Ongoing compliance and documentation monitoring
